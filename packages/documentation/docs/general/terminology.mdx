title: Terminology
---

Regardless of which implementation you go for, the terminology surrounding footnotes remain the same:

- **References:** these are the links within the main content that points to a footnote at the bottom.
- **Footnotes:** these are the actual footnotes at the bottom of the page.
- **Back links:** these are the links within the footnotes to go back to their reference.
